# The Magic System

Magic in this world flows from the Leylines, invisible streams of energy that connect all living things.

## Schools of Magic

### Elemental Magic
Control over the basic elements: fire, water, earth, and air.

### Arcane Magic
Raw magical energy that can be shaped by the will of the caster.

### Divine Magic
Power granted by the gods to their faithful followers.

### Nature Magic
Magic drawn from the natural world and its spirits.

## Learning Magic

To become a mage, one must first attune themselves to the Leylines. This process can take years of meditation and study.

## Magic Items

Certain objects can store and channel magical energy:
- Wands and staves
- Enchanted crystals
- Blessed amulets
- Runic stones
