# Dragon Lore

Dragons are ancient and powerful creatures that have inhabited the world since the dawn of time.

## Types of Dragons

### Fire Dragons
Fire dragons breathe flames hot enough to melt steel. They prefer to live in volcanic regions.

### Ice Dragons
Ice dragons command the power of frost and snow. They make their homes in the frozen north.

### Storm Dragons
Storm dragons control lightning and thunder. They are often found in mountainous regions where storms are frequent.

## Dragon Society

Dragons are highly intelligent and solitary creatures. They only gather for important ceremonies or in times of great danger.

## Famous Dragons

- **Scaleborn the Ancient** - The oldest known dragon, said to be over 10,000 years old
- **Frostfang** - Guardian of the Northern Wastes
- **Thunderwing** - Master of the Sky Peaks
