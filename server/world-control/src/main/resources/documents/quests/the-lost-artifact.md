# The Lost Artifact

## Overview
A powerful artifact has been stolen from the Temple of Ages and must be recovered before it falls into the wrong hands.

## Objectives

1. Investigate the theft at the Temple of Ages
2. Follow the trail of the thieves through the Dark Forest
3. Infiltrate the Bandit Stronghold
4. Retrieve the artifact from the Bandit Leader
5. Return the artifact to the High Priest

## Rewards

- 1000 gold coins
- Experience: 5000 XP
- Item: Ring of Protection
- Reputation: +50 with Temple of Ages

## Difficulty
Level 15 recommended

## Notes
Be prepared for combat. The Bandit Stronghold is heavily guarded.
