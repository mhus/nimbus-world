# Dragon Slayer

## Overview
A rogue dragon has been terrorizing the villages in the northern mountains. The king has issued a call for brave adventurers to slay the beast.

## Objectives

1. Speak with the Village Elder in Northwind
2. Gather information about the dragon's lair
3. Obtain dragon-slaying equipment from the Royal Armory
4. Travel to the Dragon's Peak
5. Defeat the dragon
6. Return to the king for your reward

## Rewards

- 5000 gold coins
- Experience: 15000 XP
- Item: Dragonscale Armor
- Title: Dragon Slayer
- Reputation: +100 with Northern Kingdom

## Difficulty
Level 25+ recommended
Party of 4-6 adventurers suggested

## Warnings
This is an extremely dangerous quest. Dragons are powerful creatures that should not be underestimated.
Ensure your party is well-equipped and has healing potions.
